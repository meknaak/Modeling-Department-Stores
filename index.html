Modeling Department Stores

This website is for the Modeling Department Stores project. This project utilizes historical records, including photraphs and architectural drawings, of department stores in Britain and Germany at the turn of the 20th Century to create 3D models of the stores. The models stand in for buildings which have been destroyed or rebuilt. The result of this is that even where buildingds exist at the same addresses, they no longer resemble the historic stores.

To see the locations, visit the Stores Map
